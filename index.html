My name is Kevin Tate and I am a Web Developer living in New York City. I have almost 4 years of experience in the software development industry in multiple roles. After graduating from The Ohio State University I began my career as a Release Manager and Scrum Master where I learned process engineering, leadership, management, and communication skills. I now am technically oriented as a developer with my main skills being the languages of the web: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; including libraries and frameworks of the 3 such as Handlebars, Sass/SCSS, jQuery and React.

My experience includes a 3 year stint at Abercrombie & Fitch in Columbus, Ohio in multiple roles within the IT department and recently a 6 month contract as a web developer for Hudson's Bay Company in New York City. I'm currently working on multiple freelance projects and looking to find my next full time role.

For fun I like to travel, read, play sports, watch movies, and be a tourist in my own City. As a Broadway nerd, I leave you with a quote from my favorite musical that describes how I like to look at and live life:

" Look around, look around, at how lucky we are to be alive right now.
History is happening in Manhattan and we just happen to be in the greatest city in the world "
- Hamilton the musical, lyrics by Lin-Manuel Miranda

Thank you for taking the time to check out my website :)

Looking forward to hearing from you,
Kevin
