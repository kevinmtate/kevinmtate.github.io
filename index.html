about me.

My name is Kevin Tate and I am a Web Developer living in New York City. I have almost 4 years of experience in the software development industry in multiple roles. After graduating from The Ohio State University, I began my career as a Release Manager and Scrum Master where I learned process engineering, leadership, management, and communication skills. I now am technically oriented as a developer with my main skills being the languages of the web: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (and of course jQuery); including libraries and frameworks of the 3 such as Handlebars, Sass/SCSS, and React.

My experience includes a 3 year stint at Abercrombie & Fitch in Columbus, Ohio in multiple roles within the IT department and recently a 6 month contract as a web developer for Hudson's Bay Company in New York City. I'm currently working on multiple free lance projects and looking to find my next full time role.
